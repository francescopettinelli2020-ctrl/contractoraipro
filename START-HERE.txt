========================================================
  CONTRACTORAIPRO — GET TANYA LIVE TONIGHT
  No coding. About 30 minutes. Just clicking.
========================================================

You have 3 short jobs. Do them in order.

--------------------------------------------------------
JOB 1 — Get your API key (the thing that powers Tanya)
~5 minutes
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to:  console.anthropic.com
2. Sign up or log in.
3. Click "Billing" on the left → add a credit card →
   add $20 of credit. (Tanya costs a fraction of a cent
   per question; $20 lasts a long time.)
4. Click "API keys" → "Create Key" → name it "Tanya".
5. COPY the key it shows you (it starts with sk-ant-...).
   Paste it into a notes app for a minute. You'll use it
   once in Job 2. (If you lose it, just make a new one.)

--------------------------------------------------------
JOB 2 — Put the website online
~20 minutes, all in your web browser
--------------------------------------------------------
First, GitHub (this just holds the files):
1. Go to:  github.com  → Sign up (free).
2. Top-right "+" → "New repository".
3. Name it:  contractoraipro  → click "Create repository".
4. On the next page click the link "uploading an existing file".
5. Drag in EVERYTHING from this folder:
      - index.html
      - the "api" folder (with tanya.js inside)
      - vercel.json
   (Drag the whole lot together; keep the api folder as-is.)
6. Click "Commit changes".

Now Vercel (this makes it a real live site):
7. Go to:  vercel.com  → "Sign Up" → "Continue with GitHub"
   → click Authorize.
8. Click "Add New..." → "Project".
9. Find "contractoraipro" in the list → click "Import".
10. BEFORE you deploy, click to open "Environment Variables".
    Add ONE:
        Name:   ANTHROPIC_API_KEY
        Value:  (paste your sk-ant-... key from Job 1)
    Click "Add".
11. Click "Deploy". Wait about 1 minute.
12. You'll get a link like  contractoraipro.vercel.app
    Open it. Tap the glowing orb (bottom-right).
    The badge at the top should say "● Tanya is live".
    Ask her: "Ballpark a 600 square foot patio."
    She answers from your real Milton Stone pricing. 🎉

--------------------------------------------------------
JOB 3 — Put it on your phone like an app
~1 minute
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Open your Vercel link on your phone (Safari or Chrome).
2. Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen".
3. It now opens full-screen like a real app. Talk to Tanya
   with the voice orb (works in Chrome, Safari, or Edge).

--------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT
--------------------------------------------------------
• Keep your sk-ant- key private. It's already safe on the
  server (you put it in the Environment Variable), never in
  the website itself.
• The supplier/manufacturer prices in here are your
  CONFIDENTIAL contractor pricing. Don't share the link
  with customers. Keep it for you and your team.
• Stuck? Hand this whole folder + these steps to any web
  developer (or a Fiverr freelancer, ~$50). It's a
  30-minute job for them.
